## What it does

A suite of interactive Power BI dashboards built for executive review:

- **Revenue & performance** — trends over time with drill-down by segment.
- **Customer behaviour & churn signals** — early indicators surfaced for action.
- **Segmentation** — cohorts and categories that drive planning decisions.
- **Time-series visualisation** — dynamic DAX measures expose seasonal and quarterly shifts.

## Key decisions & trade-offs

- **DAX-driven, not snapshot exports** — measures recompute as filters change, so one report serves many questions.
- **Executive-first layout** — the headline numbers read in seconds, with detail a click away.
