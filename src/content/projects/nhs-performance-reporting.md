## What it does

Compliance-grade reporting delivered inside a live NHS service:

- Monitored **wait times, activity trends, and operational KPIs**.
- Extracted and summarised data from **multiple clinical sources** for ad-hoc and periodic reporting.
- Tracked **data-accuracy KPIs and audit readiness** across the Adult Diabetes Service.
- Built executive-ready Excel and Power BI dashboards for senior leadership review.

## Key decisions & trade-offs

- **Compliance first** — accuracy and audit trails took priority over visual flourish.
- **Multi-source extraction** — reconciling several clinical systems into one trustworthy view.
